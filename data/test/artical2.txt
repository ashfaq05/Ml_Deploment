Filmmaker Anand Gandhi reveals Sushant Singh Rajput's last seen on Whatsapp was Sunday 9:15 am as he pens a heartfelt eulogy
Sushant Singh Rajput's sudden demise left his fans and B-town stars in shock. The star passed away by suicide on June 14th at his Bandra residence. Many of the actor's friends have been talking about their fondest memories with the actor after his death. Recently, filmmaker Anand Gandhi penned down a heartfelt eulogy for the 'Kedarnath' actor. In his post, he revealed that Sushant Singh Rajput's last seen on Whatsapp was 9:15 am.
